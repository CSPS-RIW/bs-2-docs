Range of learners

Diverse learners with different needs and perspectives will take this course and engage in it differently. These differences may include:

  • mental health histories and experiences: For example, you may have experienced mental health challenges, intergenerational trauma, or both.
  • position within the government: You may be a manager responsible for the psychological health and safety of employees, an employee concerned about a colleague or yourself, or all three.
  • experiences of psychological trauma inside or outside of work: You may be experiencing the mental health impacts of racism, sexism, homophobia, ableism, or other forms of oppression and their intersections.
  • resources and benefits: You may be a permanent employee with employment security and mental health benefits for you and your family, or you may be a temporary employee with fewer mental health benefits.

Each of us experiences mental health within and outside of the workplace differently. Better appreciation of this reality can enhance our consideration and empathy for others, as well as encourage gentleness and understanding for ourselves.
