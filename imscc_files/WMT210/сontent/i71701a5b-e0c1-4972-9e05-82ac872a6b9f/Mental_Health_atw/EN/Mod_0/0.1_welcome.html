Welcome!

Welcome to the course Mental Health and the Workplace: Raising Awareness. 

This is an introductory course designed for public servants interested in becoming more aware about mental health in the workplace. The course highlights the importance of shining a light on the topic of mental health and work. It discusses and contextualizes mental health through diverse workplace scenarios and experiences, presents the signs and symptoms of mental health issues and the various coping and support strategies, and provides tools and resources for employees and management.

Note

You will continue to have access to this course even after you complete it. Feel free to return to the course frequently to gauge your mental health or to discover different strategies for coping during periods of mental health stress. Also note that you may complete this course at your own pace and are not required to complete the whole course at once.
