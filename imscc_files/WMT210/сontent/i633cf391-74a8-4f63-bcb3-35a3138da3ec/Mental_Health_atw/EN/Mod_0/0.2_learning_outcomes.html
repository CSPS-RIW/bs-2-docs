Learning outcomes

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

  • describe mental health in the context of workplace interactions
  • recognize the signs and symptoms of mental health issues
  • understand how to become better aware of your mental health
  • identify where to seek support and what principles to keep in mind when supporting others
  • help colleagues or employees contribute to positive workplace interactions and mental health in the workplace
  • access relevant resources and information on mental health supports
